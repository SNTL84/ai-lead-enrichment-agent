## 🔴 Problem

SDRs and sales teams doing B2B cold outreach spend **30–45 minutes per lead** manually: finding the right founder on LinkedIn, checking funding stage on Crunchbase, writing a personalized opener from scratch. At 20 leads a day, that's **8+ hours of research** before a single message is sent. Most of that research is repetitive, templated, and ripe for automation.

---

## 🛠️ What I Built

**AI Lead Enrichment Agent** — a 100% browser-based tool that enriches startup/B2B leads automatically:

- **Founder names & LinkedIn profiles** — pulled automatically from company name
- **Funding stages & investment rounds** — no Crunchbase tab-switching required
- **Personalized outreach hooks** — AI-generated opening lines specific to each company
- **Industry tags & signals** — for smarter segmentation
- **Bulk processing** — enrich multiple leads in one session
- **Export to CSV** — download and paste directly into your outreach tool
